Research Interest Profile: onda

(Identity, affiliation, and career context are held in the private collaborator

Top priority (dual focus):
  (A) CMB B-mode polarization & primordial gravitational waves
      - Inflation verification, tensor-to-scalar ratio r, consistency relation
      - Reionization bump, recombination bump, delensing
      - LiteBIRD science goal as the central target
  (B) LiteBIRD satellite instrumentation and in-orbit systematics
      - Cosmic-ray impact evaluation, onboard signal-processing hardware design,
        EMI/RFI interference, in-orbit calibration strategy

Core science topics (CMB / cosmology):
- Inflation models: single-field slow-roll, alpha-attractors, axion monodromy,
  Starobinsky-type, and their tensor spectrum predictions
- Primordial gravitational waves: current r upper bounds (BICEP/Keck),
  forecasts for r ~ 1e-3 targets, consistency relation, non-Gaussianity
- Reheating, preheating, and their imprint on CMB scales
- CMB polarization power spectra (EE / BB / TE), lensing B-modes, delensing
- CMB foregrounds: synchrotron, thermal dust, anomalous microwave emission;
  component separation methods (ILC, NILC, SMICA, Commander)
- Cosmic birefringence (isotropic and anisotropic rotation), axion-like
  particles as coupling mediators
- Statistical isotropy, parity violation, large-scale anomalies

Instrumentation / systematics (LiteBIRD-relevant working line):
- TES / bolometer / MKID cryogenic detectors and readout (DfMux, multiplexing)
- Cosmic-ray systematic effects on bolometers and their impact on B-mode
  recovery (Stever et al. 2021 JCAP line)
- EMI / RFI / micro-vibration interference evaluation on cryogenic detectors
  (XRISM Resolve heritage applied to LiteBIRD)
- Onboard signal processing and data compression for bolometer arrays
  (Tominaga et al. 2022 JLTP line)
- HWP (half-wave plate) calibration, polarization angle systematics
- 1/f noise mitigation, scan-strategy design
- Beam systematics, pointing reconstruction, sidelobe pickup
- End-to-end forecasting and bias / leakage analysis for B-mode science

Related experiments (read for comparison and context):
- Ground-based CMB: Simons Observatory, CMB-S4, POLARBEAR, BICEP/Keck, SPT, ACT
- Space: Planck legacy re-analyses, PICO concept
- Complementarity between ground and space for primordial GW searches

Related theory (hep-ph / gr-qc side of the digest):
- Primordial GW generation mechanisms beyond standard inflation
- Axion-like particles and cosmic birefringence phenomenology
- Effective field theory of inflation and EFT of large-scale structure (light)
- Stochastic GW backgrounds overlap (PTA, LIGO, CMB B-mode complementarity)
